Use conditions

BurrowScope is provided for non-profit research, conservation, and field inspection work.

The inference engine runs locally in this browser through ONNX Runtime. Your images, masks, counts, and exported results are not sent to a cloud service by this app.

Review the outputs before using them in reports. The tool supports per-image burrow segmentation and visual inspection.
